## Key Takeaways
- Establish a repeatable weekly operating cadence to monitor AI quality post-launch
- AI quality requires continuous measurement and intervention, not set-and-forget deployment
- Focus on three pillars: accuracy, safety, and customer trust

## Guardrails & Anti-patterns
- **Anti-pattern:** Deploying AI without an operational review loop; quality degrades over time through data drift, user behavior changes, and edge cases
- **Guardrail:** Weekly cadence prevents quality issues from compounding and maintains stakeholder confidence
