## Key Takeaways
- Establish pre-production testing environment for AI-driven support tools before customer exposure
- Test scope includes: bots, prompts, automations, and routing logic
- Prevents customer-facing failures and reduces support escalations from AI errors

## Guardrails & Anti-patterns
- **Anti-pattern:** Deploying AI models, prompt changes, or routing rules directly to production without staged testing
- **Guardrail:** Mandatory sandbox validation before any AI feature reaches customer interactions
