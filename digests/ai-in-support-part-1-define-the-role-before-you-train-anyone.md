# AI in Support, Part 1: Define the role before you train anyone

**Source:** https://1fernandoduarte.substack.com/p/ai-in-support-part-1-define-the-role  
**Date:** November 17, 2025  
**Category:** 1. AI in Customer Support; 6. Support Leadership & Team Management  
**Recency Score:** 0-6 months (full weight)

## Key Takeaways
- Establish explicit role definition for AI tools *before* conducting tool training
- Clarify organizational purpose and intended use cases to prevent misalignment between team expectations and implementation

## Guardrails & Anti-patterns
- **Anti-pattern:** Rolling out AI tooling without pre-defined scope leads to inconsistent adoption and unclear accountability
- **Guardrail:** Define "what AI is for" at the organizational level prior to "how to use it" at the individual level
