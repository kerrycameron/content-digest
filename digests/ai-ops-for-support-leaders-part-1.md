## Key Takeaways
- Intent data serves as foundational infrastructure for AI-driven support operations
- Treating intent data as a product (with governance, versioning, quality standards) enables scalable AI implementation
- Data foundation precedes effective AI ops deployment

## Frameworks / Models
- **Intent Data as Product Framework**: Establish intent data with product-like characteristics including documentation, versioning, quality metrics, and stakeholder ownership rather than treating it as a byproduct

## Guardrails & Anti-patterns
- **Anti-pattern:** Deploying AI solutions without first establishing clean, well-governed intent data infrastructure
- **Guardrail:** Require data governance structure before scaling AI implementations
