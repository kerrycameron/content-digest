## Key Takeaways
- Systematize AI failure analysis by converting incidents into labeled defect categories
- Establish ownership and remediation paths for each defect type
- Use defect taxonomy as the operational foundation for continuous AI improvement

## Frameworks / Models
- **Defect Taxonomy Structure:** Convert support AI failures into:
  - Classification labels (defect type/category)
  - Ownership assignment (responsible team/system)
  - Fix paths (remediation steps and prevention logic)

## Guardrails & Anti-patterns
- Avoid ad-hoc incident response; require systematic categorization of every AI failure
- Anti-pattern: Tracking defects without clear ownership accountability
- Anti-pattern: Identifying failure types without documented fix paths
